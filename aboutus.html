GAME RULES

How to Play BouncingBall

Objective

Complete each level by collecting the required items. Each level has different objectives - stars, eggs, and portals!

Controls

Move the Log: Use your mouse to move the log (paddle) left and right, or use Arrow Keys (Left/Right)
Launch the Ball: Press SPACE to launch the ball upward

Game Mechanics

  • Stars: Collect golden stars to complete levels
  • Eggs: Some levels require collecting eggs (Level 2 and 4)
  • Switch & Portal: Some levels have a switch that opens a portal - enter the portal to win (Level 3 and 4)
  • Grass Blocks: Bounce off green grass blocks - they don't hurt you
  • Sand Blocks: Avoid brown sand blocks - hitting them means game over!
  • Ball Physics: The ball bounces realistically off all surfaces

Losing Conditions

  • Hit a sand block - instant game over!
  • Ball goes over the log before you've hit it
  • Ball falls off the bottom of the screen
  • You take too long (15 seconds) to hit the log after launching

Tips to Win

  • Keep your eyes on the ball and position the log accordingly
  • React quickly - the game gets faster on higher levels!
  • Focus on collecting stars, not bouncing endlessly
  • The longer you play a level, the more challenging it becomes
  • If you lose, you restart the same level - no progress is lost
